FAQ

Short, clear answers to the questions clients ask most.

How do you charge for work?

For small jobs we use per-incident pricing. For ongoing support, projects or AMC, we define a clear scope and fixed or monthly pricing before we start.

Do you support remote as well as onsite?

Yes. Most issues are solved remotely. For network, hardware and rollout work, onsite visits are planned as needed.

What about downtime during migration?

We plan cutovers during low-usage windows, keep fall-back options ready and communicate clearly to users so downtime is minimal.

What does your AMC / managed IT cover?

Proactive health checks, patching guidance, incident handling, user management, periodic networking & security reviews and advisory on improvements.
